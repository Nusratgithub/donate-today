Our Donate Plan

Our intentions are to provide Emergency Aid to those in need and help the flood victims of Bangladesh with food packs and essential items.
We plan to help the most vulnerable people to access nutritious food and essential items as soon as possible.

Food for a family

Food is a basic necessity for survival and also the key to a healthy life. Without regular healthy meals children struggle to go to school, carers struggle to work and communities are left battling to survive.children are always the ones most affected by basic healthcare.

Shelter for a family

Flood is an integral part of Bangladesh and so is the suffering of the people living in flood-prone areas.Flood shelters in three locations along three major rivers were visited, and communities were consulted to address some selected issues regarding the shelters.

Education for achild

The importance of education cannot be overestimated, as it is the key to a bright and successful future.Our focus is to provide children with the opportunity to learn and develop, so they can grow up to be an integral and healthy part of their communities.
